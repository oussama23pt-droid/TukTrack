Privacy Policy

This privacy policy applies to the TukTrack app developed by WARRIORMIRAGE UNIP LDA.

Effective Date: 20/05/2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you use TukTrack:

  • Email address and account credentials
  • Business name, NIF, and contact details
  • Vehicle and driver information you add
  • Real-time GPS location of vehicles
  • Payment information (processed securely via Stripe)
  • Trip and route data

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide fleet management services
  • To process payments for subscriptions
  • To track vehicle locations for fleet operators
  • To send notifications about your account
  • To improve our services

3. Third Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Firebase (Google) — authentication, database, and analytics
  • Stripe — secure payment processing
  • Mapbox — maps and location services
  • Google Play Services — app distribution

4. Location Data

TukTrack collects real-time GPS location data of registered vehicles. This data is only accessible to the fleet manager who owns the account. Location data is used solely for fleet tracking purposes.

5. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on Google Firebase servers. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

6. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and data by contacting us.

7. GDPR Rights

If you are in the EU, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to data processing

8. Children's Privacy

TukTrack is not intended for users under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect data from children.
